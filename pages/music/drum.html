Drum Blocks


Blocks in this palette are for making commonly found percussion sounds used in music. Some of these include sounds make by some animals as well. Anywhere a Pitch block can be used—e.g., inside of the matrix or a Note value block—a Drum Sample block can also be used instead. Following listing gives the nature of the sound each block is produced
