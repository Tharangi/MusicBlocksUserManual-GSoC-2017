Pitch Blocks


The Pitch Palette contains blocks used to create and modify pitches used by the Note block.


Raises any contained notes by one step in a 12-step scale (pitch space). The Sharp block can be nestled inside other Sharp blocks to raise a pitch or collection of contained pitches even further.
Lowers any contained notes by one step in a 12-step scale (pitch space). The Flat block can be nestled inside other Flat blocks to lower a pitch or collection of contained pitches even further.
Transposes any contained notes by an integral step in a 12-step scale (pitch space).
Inverts a pitch around another pitch.
Contains the current transposition (the default is 0).
